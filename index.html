🥾Trails

A short list of hikes nearby, ranging from easy gorge walks to longer loops in the national forest.

  • Watkins Glen State Park — Gorge Trail ~10 min south · ~2 mi · moderate (many stairs) Iconic stone path past 19 waterfalls and 200-ft cliffs. The gorge trail is closed in winter; rim trails stay open. Park info → Directions →
  • Havana Glen Park — Eagle Cliff Falls ~15 min south (Montour Falls) · short walk · easy A small county park with a quick walk in to a 41-ft waterfall — good if you want a waterfall without the Watkins Glen crowds. Park info → Directions →
  • Finger Lakes National Forest — Interloken / Burnt Hill ~25–30 min east (Hector) · 3–12 mi options · easy to moderate The only national forest in NY. Mix of woods, meadows, and pasture; the Interloken Trail runs ~12 mi north–south, with shorter loops like Burnt Hill (~3.8 mi). USDA Forest Service → Directions →
  • Catharine Valley Trail multiple access points · flat · easy Year-round rail trail through Schuyler County for walking, biking, snowshoeing. Free. Trail info → Directions →
  • Queen Catharine Marsh Loop ~10 min south of Watkins Glen · ~7 mi · level Quiet wildlife-management-area trail through marsh and woods — good for birding. DEC info → Directions →
  • Seneca Lake State Park — Lakefront Trail ~45 min north (Geneva) · ~4.3 mi · easy/paved Flat lakefront walk. Pair with a stroll through downtown Geneva. Park info → Directions →

🚗Day Trips — Nearby Towns

Pick a town for the day. All within roughly an hour. Tap "Directions" for live travel time.

  • Watkins Glen ~10 min south The state park gorge, a walkable harbor, Watkins Glen International racetrack in season, and the southern anchor of the Seneca Lake Wine Trail. Visitor info → Directions →
  • Hammondsport ~35–40 min west · Keuka Lake Small lakeside village square. Glenn H. Curtiss Museum (early aviation & motorcycles) and Pleasant Valley Wine Co (1860, U.S. Bonded Winery No. 1) anchor a half-day visit. Visitor info → Directions →
  • Corning ~40 min south The Corning Museum of Glass is the headline draw (glassmaking demos, hot-glass workshops, 50,000+ objects). The Gaffer District has a walkable downtown; the Rockwell Museum covers American art. Gaffer District → Museum of Glass → Directions →
  • Ithaca ~50–60 min east · Cayuga Lake Cornell University, the Ithaca Commons (pedestrian downtown), and three big gorges nearby — Taughannock, Buttermilk, and Robert H. Treman state parks. Visitor info → Directions →
  • Geneva ~45 min north · north end of Seneca Lake Walkable historic downtown on the lakefront, 19th-century architecture, lakefront state park, and the start of the Seneca Lake Wine Trail's east side. Visitor info → Directions →
  • Penn Yan ~40 min west · Keuka Lake Compact downtown with shops, lakefront access, and a different (Y-shaped) Finger Lake to explore. Easy half-day. Visitor info → Directions →
